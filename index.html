About Us

Meet Eric & Tamika

We're a husband-and-wife team who fell in love with cruising — not the fancy Instagram version, but the real version. The buffets at 2am, the port adventures that don't go as planned, and the sunsets that make you forget you were ever on land.

Tamika handles the research, the editing, and making sure we don't miss a thing. Eric brings the experience and the stories you won't find in any travel guide.

CruiseBettr is about real talk — what worked, what didn't, and whether a cruise is actually worth it. We don't sugarcoat it.
